Afrit 4 Amstelveen-Oost,
Beneden bij verkeerslichten rechts
1e verkeerslicht rechts richting Uithoorn.
Over metrospoorbaan linksaf Beneluxbaan op.

English:

Note:
Euro

Travel directions:
A10 Coentunnel,A4 towards Den Haag
A9 towards Utrecht Exit 4 Amstelveen-East,
Down at the traffic lights turn right
1st traffic light right towards Uithoorn.
Cross the metro railway left on to Beneluxbaan.
At traffic lights at the slight right corner turn left on to the Gondel.
Follow this road until the roundabout, then turn right.
After 150 meters cross the railway to get to the Brink.
After AH turn right, unloading place right around the corner.

Unloading place:
Load right
Drive away:
Drive around, cross the railway again at the 1st traffic lights
