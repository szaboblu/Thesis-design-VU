English:
Travel directions:
A10 in the direction of Coentunnel,
exit S105,
turn left at the top (Jan van Galen straat)
After the Food Center roundabout, turn right (Frederik Hendrikstraat).
1st traffic light turn left (De Clercqstraat),
before the bridge turn right Da Costakade),
after 100 mtr Albert Heijn shop unloading place right at housenr 52

Unloading place:
Tight unloading area difficult to reach. (load right, you hang a bit to the left.)
Drive away:
Mandatory route due to subsidence: 1st bridge turn left, straight ahead, right keep left, end left
